About Me

Welcome to my digital portfolio! I'm Somya Sachan, a passionate and results-driven professional with over 4 years of experience in the healthcare sector. My journey began with a profound interest in utilizing technology to enhance healthcare processes and provide exceptional customer service to patients and practitioners alike.

Creative Problem Solver

As a strategic account manager at eClinicalWorks EHR, I have successfully managed administrative work for over 135 practitioners, including ENT, Cardio, and Multispeciality. I take pride in implementing needs-specific solutions, such as online scheduling during the COVID-19 pandemic, which contributed to a 25% increase in revenue. My expertise in electronic medical records software, eClinicalWorks, has streamlined workflows and ensured seamless operations for healthcare practices.

Innovative Data Scientist

My love for technology extends to the world of data science. Through my capstone projects, I have designed and developed a Sales Forecasting System to predict sales trends and a Music Recommendation System to create personalized playlists for music enthusiasts. Currently, I'm working on a Healthcare IT project, aiming to develop an efficient ticket generation system for the electronic Medication Administration Record (eMAR).

Academic Excellence

I am constantly seeking opportunities to learn and grow. Pursuing a post-graduation certification in Artificial Intelligence Design and Implementation at Durham College has fueled my passion for AI and its applications. As an honor roll student, I thrive on exploring cutting-edge solutions to real-world challenges.

Beyond Work

When I'm not immersed in the world of healthcare and data science, I enjoy contributing to the community as a program coordinator. Organizing fundraisers and college events have honed my leadership skills and ability to work harmoniously with diverse teams.

I believe in continuous growth and embrace challenges as opportunities to learn. My digital portfolio showcases a selection of my projects and accomplishments, reflecting my commitment to excellence and innovation. I am excited to connect with like-minded individuals, collaborate on new ventures, and make a positive impact in the world through technology and creativity.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Let's explore new horizons together!
